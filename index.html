Provenance and Vision

Blob team's co-founder Eloc, previously Pudgy Penguin CTO, inscribed an interactive generative art by Nuro on the sat and used it as a parent to etch the EPIC Rune token on May 4th, paying tribute to Bitcoin pioneer Hal Finney.

The vision of EPIC Rune is to become the most epic meme token in the Bitcoin ecosystem and naturally become the top meme coin as the ecosystem grows.
